ABOUT THE PROJECT

A COMMUNITY OF GENERATIVE ART

Ever-growing online museum

The purpose of Generative Art Museum is to create a community where artists and engineers from all around the world can share their art by uploading posts, creating albums and making a fully customizable profile, where they keep their online presence as artists. Users will also be able to like others' posts, add comments and ahare any piece of art they find interesting. This online museum will constantly expand its gallery and community, becoming an online place where everyone can participate, either as a visitor or as an artist.
